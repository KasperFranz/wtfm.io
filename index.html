Write the F!@#$%^ Manual

Writing documentation sucks. Not writing documentation sucks more.

From making a business case for incorporating documentation into the product development workflow to creating functional, test-driven documentation; Write the F!@#$%^ Manual is the handbook for learning how to write high quality documentation without sacrificing productivity — or sanity.

Who Are You?

My name is Zachary Flower.

I have been a software developer for my entire adult life — and some of my non-adult life — and in that time I’ve had the good fortune of working with teams of all sizes. Despite the size of any organization, I have come to learn that many productivity problems are really communication problems, and often focus on ways to improve engineering transparency through technical documentation. Unmaintainable legacy code, knowledge silos, and slow developer onboarding processes are all drastically improved by keeping clean, up to date documentation on the inner-workings of any technical process.

Where's the Book?

Write the F!@#$%^ Manual is currently a work in progress — progress which you can track directly on GitHub.
